Who We Are

MEGA STORE is a brand new app store platform launched in March 2026 with a clear mission: to create a simpler, safer, and more enjoyable way for users to discover and download applications. We are a fresh startup founded by a passionate team of developers and designers who believe the app discovery experience needs to be reimagined.

Unlike established platforms that have become cluttered and overwhelming, MEGA STORE starts with a clean slate. We are building from the ground up with modern principles: quality over quantity, user privacy by default, and genuine developer relationships.

"We are just getting started. Every download, every review, every suggestion helps us build the app store we all deserve."

As a new platform, we are carefully curating our initial catalog to ensure every app meets our standards. We are starting small but thinking big—focusing on building trust with our first users rather than chasing numbers.
