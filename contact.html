When & How to Call?

Best Time to Call:

  • Monday - Saturday: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM (Business Hours)
  • Sunday: Emergency cases only - 12:00 PM to 4:00 PM
  • Lunch Break: 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM (avoid calling)

Before You Call:

  • Keep your Order Number ready (if order related)
  • Remember your registered mobile number
  • Prepare notes to clearly explain your problem
  • Keep pen and paper handy to note down details

Call is Best For:

Urgent Issues Order Problems Payment Failures Delivery Issues Account Locked

Average Response Time: Instant (connect in 2-5 minutes)

chat

When & How to Message/Chat?

Availability:

  • 24/7: You can send messages anytime
  • Reply Time: Usually within 2-4 hours (during business hours)
  • Night Messages: Messages sent after 9:00 PM will be replied next morning

How to Write Effective Messages:

  • Keep subject clear: Be specific like "Order #12345 - Delivery Issue"
  • Provide details: When it happened, what is the problem, what you expect
  • Attach screenshots: Photos of error messages or issues
  • Keep it short and clear: But include all important information

Message is Best For:

General Queries Product Questions Feedback/Suggestions Price Inquiries Non-Urgent Issues

Average Response Time: 2-4 hours

email

When & How to Email?

Best Time:

  • Business Hours: 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM
  • Response Time: Guaranteed reply within 24 hours
  • Weekend Emails: Will be replied on Monday

Email Format:

  • Subject Line: Clear and specific - "[Order #12345] Return Request"
  • Body: Professional but friendly tone
  • Attachments: Include invoices, screenshots, photos
  • Contact Info: Provide your phone number and alternate email

Email is Best For:

Detailed Issues Documentation Required Official Communication Bulk Orders Business Partnerships

Average Response Time: 24 hours
